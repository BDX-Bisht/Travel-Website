Itinerary

The best time of the year to visit Almaty is in the summer months from June to September. The temperature during this time is ideal for exploring the city and also best for indulging in adventure activities. September to February is extremely cold and March to May are piping hot temperatures.

The destinations in Almaty that can be covered in a day with our Almaty Travel Package are the Kazakhstan Museum of Arts, Museum of Folk Instruments, Almaty Big Lake, Central Mosque, Ascension Cathedral, First President Park, Rakhat Chocolate factory, Zeleny Bazar, Arbat Shopping street and Almaty Zoo

The top must-see attractions that are also a part of the Almaty Tour Package are Central Mosque, Kazakhstan Museum of Arts, Kazak Museum of Folk Instruments, Big Almaty Lake, Ascension Cathedral, First President Park, Shymbulak Ski Resort, and Kok-Tob Hill
